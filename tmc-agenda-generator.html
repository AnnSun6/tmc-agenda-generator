用法How to use

粘贴消息 → 生成。备稿 0–3 位自动加减行，IE 自动对位。

每行左侧 + 在下方插一行、× 删除该行，删后时间自动重排；改任意一行 时长，下面时间和 G/Y/R 即时重算。任意文字点开就改。

做完点 复制为图片（或下载）发群；打印 A4 按一页纸排版。 Paste message → Generate. Prepared speeches (0–3) add/remove rows automatically; IE roles align automatically.

Use + on the left of a row to insert below, × to delete (times recompute). Change any duration to recalculate times and G/Y/R below. Click any text to edit.

When done, Copy as image (or download) to share in the group; Print A4 for one-page layout.
